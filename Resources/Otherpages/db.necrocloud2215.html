Survey of the Necro Cloud Nebula, 2215

The Necro Cloud is a strange mix of a Nebula and an asteroid field towards the west of Yorktown (2,███,███ Kilometers)
Due to the copious amounts of loose rock and charged particles, ships navigating within the Necro Cloud often have sensors disrupted,
as such, all known data regarding the Necro Cloud has been collected using Megallen Probes
