About:

Yorktown is a Starbase located near the Necro Cloud Nebula (Coordinates 42.562.374.1774)

Layout:

The Starbase is constructed as a sphere 300 Kilometers in diameter, with large spiral arms for the city’s buildings

Living in Yorktown:

Life in Yorktown can be construed as quite idyllic, with the base offering the same as every M-class planet in the Federation

Starship Production/Resupply

Aside from being a Starbase, Yorktown also offers unparalleled manufacturing capabilities in the Federation, being able to produce up to 62 Starships per year
