About:

Yorktown is a Starbase located near the Necro Cloud Nebula (Coordinates 42.562.374.1774), it was created in lieu of a Planetary base to ensure the Federation remained unbiased geographically towards all member and newly inducted worlds. The Starbase currently serves as the Federation’s impromptu hub, outside of the core worlds (Earth, Vulcan, Tellar Prime, Andor, etc.) from having the remote yet advantageous position at the very edge of the Federation's western frontier. From those factors and many more, Yorktown is rated second in racial/species diversity as of the fourth quarter of 2262, just behind that of Risa

Layout:

The Starbase is constructed as a sphere 300 Kilometers in diameter, with large spiral arms for the city’s buildings

Living in Yorktown:

Life in Yorktown can be construed as quite idyllic, with the base offering the same as every M-class planet in the Federation

Starship Production/Resupply

Aside from being a Starbase, Yorktown also offers unparalleled manufacturing capabilities in the Federation, being able to produce up to 62 Starships per year
